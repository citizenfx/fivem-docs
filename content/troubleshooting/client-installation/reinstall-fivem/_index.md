---
title: Reinstalling FiveM
---

To reinstall FiveM, press: Windows Key + R then type `%localappdata%` and press Enter
![Run Box](/run-localappdata.png)

Locate and delete the `FiveM` folder.

![fivem folder](/fivem-folder.png)

Download the installer by clicking [here](https://runtime.fivem.net/client/FiveM.exe) and re-install FiveM
