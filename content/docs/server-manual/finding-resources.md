Resources can be used to add functionality to your server. You can find many of the available resources on the [Cfx Marketplace](https://marketplace.cfx.re/) or in the [FiveM Releases](https://forum.cfx.re/c/development/releases/7) / [RedM Releases](https://forum.cfx.re/c/redm-releases/60) section.

Some may be free, and others may not be. The forum and the Cfx. Marketplace have filtering functionality, so you can sort through them.

![pic](/finding-resources/resource-filtering.png)

{{% alert color="warning" title="Dependencies" %}}Some resources may have dependencies, which means they might rely on other resources, so do your research before picking one or things may not work. Resources under the [**standalone**](https://marketplace.cfx.re/discover?tag__standalone__framework=on) tag typically do not have these dependencies. {{% /alert %}}

## Responsibility

Use resources at your own risk. Resources are community-driven, and you should choose them carefully. Cfx is not responsible for any issues resulting from their use and will not provide support for community-made resources. Make sure to follow each resource's terms and conditions. Resources generally provide a way to contact the creator for support. Use the available channels if an issue arises.

{{% alert color="warning" title="Paid Resources" %}}Some resources may be paid. If you opt to pay for a resource, please contact the seller first and ask whether they are selling the resource via the [Cfx Marketplace](https://marketplace.cfx.re/) or [Tebex](https://www.tebex.io/fivem) to avoid scams. Cfx does not hold itself accountable for paid resources, so you should be diligent and careful before purchasing anything. {{% /alert %}}
